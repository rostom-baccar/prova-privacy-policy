Privacy Policy for Prova

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Your data. Your device. Your control.

Prova is built around a simple principle: you own your data.

Your food database, nutrition logs, targets, summaries, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Prova does not require an account, does not contain advertisements, does not sell personal information, and does not operate a cloud database that collects your complete nutrition history.

Prova does not request access to sensitive phone features such as your location, camera, microphone, contacts, calls, messages, calendar, or photos.

Prova includes an optional AI nutrition-estimation feature. This feature uses a Gemini API key supplied and controlled by you. Your key is stored using your device's secure credential storage, and requests are sent directly from your device to Google's Gemini API.

AI requests are not routed through a server operated by the developer.

Core privacy principles

  • Your nutrition data belongs to you.
  • Your nutrition history is stored locally by default.
  • No Prova account is required.
  • No advertising is displayed.
  • No subscription is required.
  • No sensitive phone permissions are requested.
  • Your activity is not used to create an advertising profile.
  • Your complete nutrition history is not uploaded to a developer-operated server.
  • The developer does not sell or rent user data.
  • CSV imports are processed locally.
  • Exports happen only when you deliberately initiate them.
  • AI estimates use your own Gemini API key.
  • AI requests happen only when you deliberately request an estimate.
  • Prova's core features remain usable without the optional AI feature.

1. About Prova

Prova is a personal nutrition-tracking application developed and published by Rostom Baccar.

This Privacy Policy explains how Prova processes, stores, protects, and transmits information.

Prova does not require registration, sign-in, or a developer-operated online account.

2. Information processed by Prova

Prova allows you to create and manage information including:

  • Food names and personal food-database entries;
  • Calories, protein values, quantities, and serving information;
  • Daily nutrition logs;
  • Calorie and protein targets;
  • Nutrition scores, summaries, trends, and historical statistics;
  • Ingredients and food-origin information;
  • Application settings and preferences;
  • Backup and import information;
  • Optional meal descriptions submitted for AI nutrition estimates; and
  • An optional Gemini API key provided by you.

This information is processed only to provide the features you choose to use.

3. Local storage and data ownership

Your food database, nutrition logs, targets, summaries, trends, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Nutrition information and application preferences are stored in a local SQLite database.

The developer does not operate a user-account system or cloud nutrition database that receives and stores your complete nutrition history.

You retain control over your locally stored information. You may:

  • Edit or delete individual entries;
  • Replace data through an import;
  • Export copies of your data;
  • Create a full backup;
  • Remove or replace your Gemini API key;
  • Clear Prova's application data; or
  • Delete locally stored Prova data by uninstalling the application.

Prova does not claim ownership of nutrition information, meal descriptions, food-database entries, or exported files created by you.

4. No advertising or behavioral tracking

Prova does not contain advertising.

Your nutrition activity is not used by the developer to:

  • Target advertisements;
  • Create an advertising profile;
  • Sell marketing information;
  • Recommend sponsored products; or
  • Track your activity across unrelated applications or services.

Prova does not intentionally include advertising networks, developer-operated behavioral analytics, or social-tracking features.

5. No sensitive phone permissions

Prova is designed to work without requesting access to sensitive information or features on your phone.

Prova does not request access to:

Location No precise or approximate location access
Camera No camera access
Microphone No audio recording access
Contacts No address book access
Calls No call history or phone access
Messages No SMS or messaging access
Photos and media No unrestricted photo or media-library access
Calendar No calendar access

Prova also does not request access to health records stored by other applications.

Prova uses Android's system document picker when you choose a CSV file to import. You select the specific file that Prova is allowed to read.

Using the system document picker does not give Prova unrestricted access to all files stored on your device.

Prova uses Android's native sharing interface when you choose to export or share a file. You decide which application or storage destination receives the exported file.

6. Optional AI estimates using your own API key

Prova includes an optional feature that estimates calories and protein from a meal description.

The feature works only when you:

  1. Obtain your own Gemini API key;
  2. Add that key to Prova;
  3. Enter a meal description; and
  4. Deliberately request an estimate.

When you request an estimate, Prova sends the following information directly to Google's Gemini API:

  • The meal description you entered;
  • Instructions requesting an estimated meal name, calorie value, and protein value; and
  • Your Gemini API key, which authenticates your request.

Prova does not route the request through a server operated by the developer.

The developer does not provide, own, remotely manage, or resell your API key.

Prova does not intentionally include your complete food database, complete nutrition history, historical summaries, calorie target, or protein target in an AI-estimate request.

The AI feature is optional. You can use Prova's manual logging, food database, summaries, trends, import, export, and backup functionality without adding a Gemini API key.

Information sent to Gemini is processed by Google according to the terms and privacy practices associated with your Google AI Studio or Gemini API account.

Do not enter names, confidential information, medical records, or other sensitive personal information in an AI meal description.

AI-generated estimates may be inaccurate and should be independently verified.

7. Gemini API-key storage

The Gemini API key is provided and controlled by you.

Prova stores the key using the device's secure credential-storage system. On supported Android devices, this storage is protected through Android Keystore-backed mechanisms.

The key is transmitted directly to Google's Gemini API when you request an AI estimate. It is not intentionally transmitted to or stored by the developer.

You are responsible for:

  • Protecting your Gemini API key;
  • Monitoring its usage;
  • Complying with Google's applicable terms;
  • Replacing it if it may have been compromised; and
  • Removing it when it is no longer needed.

Secure device storage reduces exposure but cannot guarantee protection against every form of device compromise.

8. CSV imports

Prova allows you to select supported CSV files through your device's document picker.

Selected files are processed locally to import food-database or nutrition-log information.

Prova does not upload imported CSV files to a server operated by the developer.

9. CSV exports and full backups

Prova can generate:

  • Food-database CSV files;
  • Nutrition-log CSV files;
  • Daily-summary CSV files; and
  • Full ZIP backups containing multiple exported files.

These files are generated locally on your device.

When you initiate an export or backup, Prova opens the device's native sharing interface. You then choose whether and where to save or send the file.

The developer does not automatically receive exported files and cannot control the application, recipient, storage provider, or service you select.

After you share a file with another application or service, that third party processes it according to its own privacy policy.

10. Information not collected by the developer

The developer does not operate a backend that collects:

  • User accounts;
  • Names or email addresses through the app;
  • Your complete nutrition history;
  • Advertising identifiers;
  • Precise or approximate location;
  • Contacts;
  • Photos or videos;
  • Camera or microphone recordings;
  • Call history;
  • SMS messages;
  • Calendar information;
  • Health records from other applications; or
  • Payment information.

The optional Gemini feature involves direct communication between your device and Google's Gemini API, as described above.

11. Device capabilities used by Prova

Prova uses only the device capabilities required to provide its stated functionality:

  • Local application storage for nutrition data;
  • Secure credential storage for the Gemini API key;
  • Android's system document picker for CSV imports;
  • Temporary file creation for exports and backups;
  • Android's native sharing interface;
  • Internet access when you request an optional Gemini estimate or open an external webpage; and
  • Haptic feedback where supported.

Internet access does not mean that Prova uploads your complete nutrition history.

Internet access is used for optional AI estimates and external webpages, such as the page where users can obtain a Gemini API key.

12. External webpages and services

Prova may open external webpages, including Google AI Studio, in your browser.

Information processed after leaving Prova is governed by the external website's or service's own terms and privacy policy.

13. Data security

Prova limits data exposure by storing your food database, nutrition history, targets, summaries, and settings locally instead of uploading them to a developer-operated cloud database.

The Gemini API key is stored using the device's secure credential-storage system.

Meal descriptions and API credentials used for AI requests are transmitted to Google's Gemini API over an encrypted HTTPS connection.

However, no electronic storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Data security may also be affected by:

  • The security of your device;
  • Your screen-lock settings;
  • Operating-system backups;
  • Malicious or compromised software;
  • Rooted or otherwise modified devices; and
  • Applications selected to receive exported files.

14. Data retention and deletion

Locally stored nutrition and application data remain on your device until you:

  • Delete individual entries;
  • Replace data through an import;
  • Clear Prova's application data;
  • Remove or replace your Gemini API key;
  • Delete exported files; or
  • Uninstall Prova.

Temporary export and backup files may remain in Prova's cache until they are deleted by Prova, the operating system, or you.

Information submitted to Gemini may be processed or retained by Google according to the terms and policies applicable to your Gemini API usage. The developer cannot retrieve or delete information retained by Google on your behalf.

Because Prova does not create a developer-operated account or upload your complete database to a developer-operated server, the developer normally has no server-side nutrition history to access or delete.

15. Children's privacy

Prova is not directed toward children under 13 or below the applicable minimum age of digital consent in the user's jurisdiction.

The optional Gemini feature requires a Google AI Studio or Gemini API credential and is subject to Google's applicable eligibility requirements and terms.
