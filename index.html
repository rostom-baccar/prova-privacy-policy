Prova

Privacy Policy

Your data. Your device. Your control.

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Prova is built around a simple principle: your nutrition data belongs to you.

Your food database, nutrition logs, targets, summaries, trends, and settings are stored locally on your device. Prova does not require an account, contain advertising, or operate a developer-controlled cloud database that stores your complete nutrition history.

Prova includes an optional AI nutrition-estimation feature. When you choose to use it, the meal description you enter is sent directly from your device to Google's Gemini API using an API key supplied by you.

1. About Prova

Prova is a personal nutrition-tracking application developed and published by Rostom Baccar.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Prova processes, where it is stored, when it may leave your device, and the choices available to you.

2. Information processed by Prova

Depending on how you use the app, Prova may process:

  • Food names and personal food-database entries
  • Calories, protein values, quantities, and serving information
  • Ingredients and store or origin information
  • Daily nutrition logs
  • Calorie and protein targets
  • Scores, summaries, trends, and historical statistics
  • Application settings and preferences
  • CSV import data
  • Export and backup information
  • Optional meal descriptions submitted for AI estimates
  • An optional Gemini API key supplied by you

This information is processed only to provide the features you choose to use.

3. Local storage and data ownership

Your food database, nutrition logs, targets, summaries, trends, and settings are stored locally in Prova's application storage, including a local SQLite database.

The developer does not operate a Prova account system or a cloud nutrition database that receives your complete nutrition history.

You retain control over your locally stored information. You can:

  • Edit or delete individual entries
  • Clear all food and nutrition data
  • Replace data through an import
  • Export copies of your data
  • Create a ZIP backup
  • Remove or replace your Gemini API key
  • Clear Prova's application storage
  • Uninstall Prova

Prova does not claim ownership of food entries, meal descriptions, nutrition logs, or exported files created by you.

4. No advertising or behavioral tracking

Prova does not contain advertising.

The developer does not use your nutrition activity to:

  • Target advertisements
  • Build an advertising profile
  • Recommend sponsored products
  • Track you across unrelated applications or services
  • Sell or rent personal information

Prova does not intentionally include advertising networks, developer-operated behavioral analytics, or social-tracking SDKs.

5. No mandatory account

Prova does not require you to create or access a Prova account.

The app does not ask you to register with a username, password, email address, phone number, social account, or OAuth provider.

A Gemini API key is a credential for Google's Gemini service. It is not a Prova account or a method of signing in to Prova.

6. Phone permissions and device capabilities

Prova does not request access to sensitive phone features such as:

  • Precise or approximate location
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Phone calls or call history
  • SMS messages
  • Calendar
  • Unrestricted access to your photo or media library
  • Health records stored by other applications

Prova uses device functionality required for its features, including:

  • Local application storage
  • Secure credential storage
  • Internet access for optional Gemini requests and external webpages
  • Android's system document picker for imports
  • Temporary file creation for exports and backups
  • Android's native sharing interface
  • Haptic feedback where supported

Using Android's document picker allows you to choose a specific file. It does not give Prova unrestricted access to all files on your device.

7. Fictional demo data

Prova includes an optional demo mode containing fictional food and nutrition information.

Demo data is provided so you can explore the food log, database, summaries, scores, and trends before creating your own entries.

Demo information is stored and processed locally. It is not real user data and is not uploaded to a developer-operated server.

Loading demo data may replace the foods and nutrition logs currently stored in Prova. The app displays a confirmation before this happens.

You can remove the demo data using Prova's local data controls.

8. Optional AI nutrition estimates

Prova includes an optional feature that estimates a meal's name, calories, and protein from a description written by you.

This feature works only when you:

  • Obtain your own Gemini API key
  • Save the key in Prova
  • Enter a meal description
  • Deliberately request an estimate

When you request an estimate, Prova sends the following directly to Google's Gemini API:

  • The meal description you entered
  • Instructions asking for an estimated meal name, calorie value, and protein value
  • Your Gemini API key, which authenticates the request

Prova does not route these requests through a server operated by the developer.

The developer does not provide, own, resell, remotely manage, or intentionally receive your Gemini API key.

Prova does not intentionally include your complete food database, complete nutrition history, historical summaries, calorie target, or protein target in the request.

Prova normally attempts to use Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite. If a model is unavailable, overloaded, unsupported, or unable to return a usable response, the app may try another compatible Gemini model.

Prova applies a local limit of 20 AI-estimate attempts per rolling 24-hour period. The usage count is stored locally on your device.

The AI feature is optional. Prova's manual logging, database, summaries, trends, demo mode, imports, exports, and backups remain available without a Gemini API key.

9. Gemini API-key storage

Your Gemini API key is supplied and controlled by you.

Prova stores the key using the device's secure credential-storage system. On supported Android devices, this uses Android Keystore-backed protection.

The key is transmitted directly to Google's Gemini API when you request an estimate. It is not intentionally transmitted to or stored by the developer.

You are responsible for:

  • Protecting your API key
  • Monitoring its usage
  • Following Google's applicable terms
  • Replacing it if it may have been exposed
  • Removing it when it is no longer needed

Secure device storage reduces exposure but cannot guarantee protection against every form of device compromise.

10. Google processing

Meal descriptions and API credentials submitted to Gemini are processed by Google according to the terms and privacy practices applicable to your Google AI Studio or Gemini API account.

Google may process or retain submitted information according to its own service terms, account configuration, and applicable policies.

The developer does not control Google's processing and cannot access or delete information retained by Google on your behalf.

Do not include names, medical records, confidential information, or other sensitive personal information in a meal description.

11. AI accuracy and safety

AI-generated estimates may be inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable for your circumstances.

AI entries are created as pending entries so that you can review and edit them.

You should independently verify nutritional estimates before relying on them.

Google's safety systems may prevent certain descriptions from being processed or may return no estimate.

12. CSV imports

Prova allows you to select supported CSV files using your device's system document picker.

Selected files are processed locally to import food-database or nutrition-log information.

You may choose to merge imported information with existing data or replace supported local data.

Prova does not automatically upload imported CSV files to a developer-operated server.

13. Exports and backups

Prova can generate:

  • Food-database CSV files
  • Nutrition-log CSV files
  • Daily-summary CSV files
  • ZIP backups containing exported information

These files are generated locally.

When you initiate an export or backup, Prova opens the device's native sharing interface. You choose which application, recipient, or storage destination receives the file.

The developer does not automatically receive exported files.

After you share a file with another application or service, that third party processes it according to its own privacy practices.

Temporary export files may remain in Prova's cache until they are removed by the app, the operating system, or you.

14. Information not collected by the developer

The developer does not operate a backend intended to collect:

  • Prova user accounts
  • Names or email addresses entered through the app
  • Your complete nutrition history
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Photos or videos
  • Camera or microphone recordings
  • Call history
  • SMS messages
  • Calendar information
  • Health records from other applications
  • Payment information
  • App-usage analytics
  • Developer-operated crash reports

The optional Gemini feature involves direct communication between your device and Google's Gemini API as described in this policy.

15. Data security

Prova limits exposure by storing your nutrition information locally rather than uploading it to a developer-controlled nutrition database.

The Gemini API key is stored using secure device credential storage.

Gemini requests are transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection.

However, no storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Security may also be affected by:

  • The security of your device
  • Your screen-lock settings
  • Operating-system backups
  • Rooted or modified devices
  • Malicious or compromised software
  • Services selected to receive exported files
  • The security of your Google account and API key

16. Data retention and deletion

Locally stored data remains on your device until you:

  • Delete entries
  • Clear all data in Prova
  • Replace information through an import
  • Remove your Gemini API key
  • Clear the application's storage
  • Delete exported files
  • Uninstall Prova

Because Prova does not create a developer-operated account or upload your complete database to a developer-operated server, the developer normally has no server-side nutrition history to access or delete.

Information submitted to Gemini may be retained by Google according to the terms and policies applicable to your Gemini API usage.

17. Children's privacy

Prova is intended for adults and is not directed toward children under 13 or below the applicable minimum age of digital consent in the user's jurisdiction.

The optional Gemini feature also requires access to a Google AI Studio or Gemini API credential and is subject to Google's eligibility requirements and terms.

Calculations, targets, scores, summaries, trends, and AI estimates are provided for general informational purposes only.

Consult a qualified healthcare professional before making significant dietary, medical, or health-related decisions.

Prova may open external webpages, including Google AI Studio and this Privacy Policy, in your browser.

Information processed after leaving Prova is governed by the external website's or service's own terms and privacy practices.
